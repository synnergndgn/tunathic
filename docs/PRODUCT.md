# Product

## Vision

Tunathic – Guitar Toolkit brings focused practice, music theory, and everyday guitar utilities into one premium, precise, and approachable mobile application. GUNDEV publishes the product under the tagline “Tune. Train. Create.”

## Target users

Tunathic is for beginner through experienced guitarists who want dependable tools, concise learning aids, and visible practice progress without switching among unrelated apps.

## Planned tools

- Guitar tuner
- Metronome
- BPM tap
- Chord library
- Scale library
- Circle of fifths
- Interval trainer
- Ear training
- Chord finder
- Capo calculator
- Practice statistics
- Daily challenges

## Product principles

- Keep timing-sensitive and practice-critical interactions fast, clear, and distraction-free.
- Work offline wherever the tool does not inherently need a network connection.
- Perform audio processing locally to protect privacy and reduce latency.
- Make accessibility and localization part of the feature, not an afterthought.
- Use respectful monetization. Core tools should not be unnecessarily locked behind subscriptions.
- Advertisements must never interrupt timing-sensitive practice tools.

## Initial scope

Tunathic launches Android-first. English is the source language and Turkish is supported from the first milestone. The product is designed around offline-first behavior. Phase 2A requests microphone access only after an explicit user action and processes transient audio locally in memory; it does not yet detect pitch or provide a working tuner.
